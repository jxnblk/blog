# About

![](/brent-jackson.jpg)

Brent Jackson lives in Brooklyn, NY with his partner and calico roommate.
Originally from Huntington, West Virginia,
he studied Sociology, Graphic Design, and Japanese Language at Marshall University.

His career in tech has led him from Web and interaction design to user experience design to front-end development.
Jackson currently works at [Gatsby][],
previously at Kickstarter, Etsy, LivingSocial, Stitch Fix, and Priceline.
He has worked on several open source projects, including [Basscss][], [CSS Stats][], [Colorable][], [Rebass][], [Styled System][], [Theme UI][], and [MDX Deck][].
